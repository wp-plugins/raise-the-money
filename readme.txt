This plugin will enable you to easily embed your Raise The Money contribution form in your WordPress website.

== Description ==

[Raise The Money](https://raisethemoney.com) makes it simple to accept political contributions on your website. You will be able to:

- Accept credit cards and ACH transfers
- Have funds deposited directly into your bank account
- Receive recurring contributions
- Flat rate of just 4.9% per transaction
- No setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden costs

Before using this plugin, you will need to [create an account with Raise The Money](https://raisethemoney.com). Once you have an account, you will be given a shortcode for your contribution form.

Your shortcode will look like this:

    [raisethemoney form="demo"]

You can place it anywhere in your website where you would like your contribution form to appear.

== Installation ==

**Overview**

1. Install the Raise The Money plugin
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Place your RTM shortcode (found in your Raise The Money account) anywhere in your website where you would like your contribution form to appear.

**Install from your WordPress website (Recommended)**

1. Sign in to your WordPress website
2. Go to "Plugins" > "Add New"
3. Search for "Raise The Money"
4. Click "Install Now"
5. Click "Activate Plugin"
6. Place your RTM shortcode (found in your Raise The Money account) anywhere in your website where you would like your contribution form to appear.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is a Raise The Money account required to use this plugin? =

Yes. The purpose of this plugin is to embed your Raise The Money contribution form in your WordPress website.

= Where can I find the shortcode? =

Sign in to your Raise The Money account, then selection "Contribution Form" from the left sidebar. You will see your shortcode under the "WordPress Shortcode" section.
